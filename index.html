Are You Ready to Play?

Rules of the Game:

  1. Play individually or in groups
  2. Select a letter of the alphabet
  3. If the letter is contained in the word, the group or individual takes another turn guessing a letter
  4. If the letter is not contained in the word, the remaining guesses will decrease
  5. Total guesses allowed are 6

*Hint* The words are randomly chosen from a list of different car makers!

Press any character key to start playing
